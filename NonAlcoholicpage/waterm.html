Watermelon Basil Cooler

Total Time: 10 minutes   Serves: 4




Ingredients

  • 1 Watermelon
  • 5-6 Fresh Basil Leaves
  • 3-4 Fresh Mint Leaves
  • Juice of half a lime


Method

  1. Chop the watermelon and remove any seeds. Chop and place into a blender with the basil and mint leaves.

  2. Taste and add more herbs if youâ€™d like to.

  3. Serve cold over ice. Enjoy!
