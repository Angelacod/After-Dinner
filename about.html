About

After Dinner is a platform that provides you with mouth-watering

dessert and drink recipes that will blow your audience away!


We have foraged through cook books and the web to find you easy

recipes that can be completed in no-time!


Whether you're a chocolate lover, fan of a cheeky cocktail,

or simply looking for fun new recipes to try: We have a recipe for you!



The Team

After Dinner was created by an enthusiastic food and drink lover,

who wants to share her passion of everything victuals with you.


Angela Oteng is a A-Level gap year student.


This website was created as part of the curriculum for the

CodeFirst:Girls Beginners coding course. CodeFirst:Girls helps to

train women gain the necessary skills in-order to code,

by offering free community coding courses across the UK.
