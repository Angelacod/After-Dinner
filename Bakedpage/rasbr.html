Raspberry Cheesecake Brownies

Total Time: 50 minutes   Serves: 5-6




Ingredients

  • 1¼ cups plus 2 tablespoons sugar, divided
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 10 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 3 eggs
  • ½ cup plus 2 tablespoons flour, divided
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup cream cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened
  • Raspberries, for topping


Method

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F with rack in upper third. Grease an 8" x 8" pan with butter, line with parchment paper.

  2. Whisk together the melted butter, 1¼ cups of sugar and cocoa powder in a medium bowl. Add the vanilla and salt. Add 2 eggs and whisk. Finally add frac12; cup of flour and baking powder and mix in. Spread batter in prepared pan.

  3. For the cream cheese layer stir together cream cheese with the softened butter, 2 tablespoons flour and 2 tablespoons sugar until smooth. Dollop mixture onto batter and use a knife to swirl mixture into brownies. Press fresh raspberries on top.

  4. Bake for around 35-40 minutes. Let cool completely before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
