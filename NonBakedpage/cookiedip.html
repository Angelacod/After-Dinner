Cookie Dough Dip

Total Time: 10 minutes   Serves: 10




Ingredients

  • ½ cup unsalted butter room temperature
  • 8 oz cream cheese room temperature
  • ⅓ cup light brown sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ cup powdered sugar
  • ¾ cup of milk chocolate chips
  • graham crackers for serving


Method

  1. Using a stand mixer (or a hand mixer + large bowl), add in butter and cream cheese and beat on medium speed until fluffy, about 2 minutes.

  2. Drop mixer speed to low and add in brown sugar, salt, vanilla extract, and powdered sugar. Mix until thoroughly combined. Using a spatula or spoon, fold in chocolate chips until chips are evenly dispersed.

  3. Serve immediately with graham crackers, nilla wafers, pretzels, or other snack food for dipping. Enjoy!
